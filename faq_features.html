Features FAQ

What is the AI Try-On feature?

The AI Try-On feature uses advanced technology to create a virtual clone of your body based on your measurements. This allows you to see how clothes will look on you before making a purchase.

How accurate is the AI Try-On feature?

Our AI Try-On feature is highly accurate, providing a realistic representation of how clothes will fit on your body. However, slight variations may occur depending on the specific garment.

Can I use the AI Try-On feature on my mobile device?

Yes, the AI Try-On feature is fully compatible with mobile devices, allowing you to enjoy a seamless shopping experience on the go.
